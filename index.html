Traxion

A suite of orchestrated tools that aims to accelerate the development of web applications.

Get Started

Frameworks orchestration

Traxion configures and manipulates multiple frameworks and provides a working and scalable application in minutes.

Enhance by generators

Traxion uses several generators and scaffolders, such as Hapify and Schematics

Powered by many

Traxion is built on top of many popular frameworks and libraries such as NestJS, Prisma, ReactAdmin, Angular, React, Nx, Hapify, Schematics, Jest, Terraform, and more.
